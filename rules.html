Rules

Werewolf is a social game of deception and deductive reasoning. Wikipedia

Objective:

  • If you are a Regular Cop (Good), kill all the Werewolves.
  • If you are a Werewolf Cop (Evil), kill all the Regular Cops.

Setup:

  • All players are secretly assigned roles. Be careful who you reveal yourself to, it's a matter of life and death!
    • Werewolves randomly assigned.
    • Cupid randomly assigned, who chooses Lovers.
    • Remaining roles are randomly assigned.

The game progresses on a day/night cycle, aligning with each business day (Eastern/Toronto Timezone):

  • 9:00am - Players discover who was killed the night before. Special roles receive any extra information in secret.
  • Players discuss who to vote to hang as a Werewolf later that evening.
  • Werewolves discuss who to unanimously vote to eat at night.
  • 4:30pm - Hard deadline for Slack messsaging votes to @Cory

A #werewolf-jpd-station Slack channel which acts as the Joistown Police Station, for public discussion amongst all active players.

Players who die are out of the game, and must not talk to remaining players about the game until it is over.

The FAQ will be updated with game questions and clarifications as needed.
