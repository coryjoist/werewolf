Roles

Regular Cop

  • Good
  • Wins if all Werewolves are killed.
  • Votes each day to hang one player they suspect to be a Werewolf.

Werewolf Cop

  • Evil
  • Wins if there are at least as many Werewolves as Regular Cops left in the game.
  • Werewolves always know the identity of all other Werewolves.
  • Votes unanimously each day to kill and eat one player.

Seer

  • Good
  • A Regular Cop with special powers.
  • Chooses another player each day, and Sees whether they are Good or Evil.

Vigilante (Hunter)

  • Good
  • A Regular Cop with special powers.
  • If the Hunter is killed, they may choose to kill any other player as they are dying.

Bodyguard

  • Good
  • A Regular Cop with special powers.
  • Chooses a player each day to protect from the Werewolves at night.
  • Can protect themself.
  • Cannot protect the same player two nights in a row.

Cupid

  • Good
  • A Regular Cop with special powers.
  • Choose two other players to become Lovers at the beginning of the game.
  • Note: Cupid will not know if whether either selected player has also already been assigned as a Werewolf.

Lovers

  • The fates of the two Lovers are forever intertwined by the Cupid.
  • The Lovers know each other's identity.
  • If one of the Lovers dies, the other Lover immediately dies of heartbreak.
  • If one Lover is a Regular Cop and the other a Werewolf, all other players must die in order for them to win.
